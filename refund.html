Refund Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Hugo Mojo sells low-priced digital products. We want checkout and delivery to feel simple and fair.

Refunds We Can Usually Approve

  • You were charged twice for the same product.
  • You paid but could not access the digital report, and we cannot fix the issue.
  • You purchased by mistake and contacted us within 7 days, before substantial use or delivery of the paid content.

Refunds We Usually Cannot Approve

  • You dislike a personality or talent result after receiving the digital report.
  • You used the paid report and then request a refund because the result did not match your expectations.
  • The request is connected to misuse, fraud, chargeback abuse, or attempted access without payment.

How to Request a Refund

Email hi@hugomojo.com with your order email, purchase date, payment provider receipt if available, and a short description of the issue.

Processing Time

We aim to respond within 2 business days. Refund timing after approval depends on the payment provider and your bank.

For access issues, email us first. Most delivery problems can be fixed quickly.
