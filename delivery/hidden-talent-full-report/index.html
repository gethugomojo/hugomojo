How to read your result

Your result is not a personality box. It is a starting clue. Ask: what do people already come to you for, even when you did not package it as a skill?

Three earning directions

  1. Content: turn your lens into posts, short videos, newsletters, or teardown notes.
  2. Service: help one person or team solve the problem your talent naturally sees.
  3. Small product: package a checklist, prompt pack, scorecard, or 7-day plan.

First tiny offer

Use this sentence:

I help [specific person] solve [small painful problem] with a [simple format] they can use this week.

Keep it narrow. If a stranger can understand it in five seconds, it is probably small enough to test.

7-day route

1

Pick one audience you already understand.

2

Write down five repeated problems you notice.

3

Choose the problem that feels most urgent and easiest to explain.

4

Turn it into a one-page checklist, memo, script, or scorecard.

5

Show it to three people and ask what feels useful or unclear.

6

Package the strongest version as a tiny paid offer.

7

Ask for one sale or one serious pre-order before expanding.
