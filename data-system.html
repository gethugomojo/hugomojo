Funnel Intelligence

Data System And Member Landing.

HugoMojo uses a lightweight event layer for static deployment. Events are pushed to dataLayer and stored locally until connected to an analytics or CRM endpoint.

Core Funnel Events

Track the anchor path.

page_view
Every page load with anonymous visitor and session id.
scanner_start
User begins the 16-question scan.
scanner_complete
All answers submitted and winning result calculated.
email_capture
Email submitted before showing the paid result wall.
checkout_click
User clicks Single Pass, Master Key, Annual Vault, or upgrade checkout.
upsell_click
User accepts the post-purchase annual upgrade.
KPI Dashboard

Minimum reporting table.

AOV
Gross checkout revenue divided by number of paid orders. Target: $17+.
Paid CR
Paid orders divided by scanner completions. Target: 8%+.
Email CR
Email captures divided by scanner completions. Target: 60%+.
Master Share
Master Key orders divided by all first purchases. Target: 65%.
LTV
Average revenue per captured user within 12 months. Target: $50+.
