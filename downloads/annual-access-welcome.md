# HugoMojo Annual Access Welcome

## What Annual Access Provides

- Private member access.
- Two plug-and-play AI toolkits every month.
- Monthly opportunity briefings.
- Quarterly path calibration.
- Priority access to new prompt assets and operating systems.

## First Member Actions

1. Save your Master Key results.
2. Choose one primary path for the next 30 days.
3. Complete the baseline resource profile.
4. Review the first monthly toolkit.
5. Submit your quarterly calibration notes when requested.

## Operating Rule

Annual Access is not a promise of income. It is a filter for high-signal AI opportunities, practical toolkits, and structured execution support.
