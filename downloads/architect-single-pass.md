# HugoMojo Architect Single Pass

## Role Signal

The Architect is built for systems, B2B automation, and high-value problem solving.

## First 7 Days

1. Choose one business type with repetitive admin work.
2. Identify three workflows that waste time.
3. Turn one workflow into an AI automation audit offer.
4. Draft a short audit message.
5. Send the message to 20 business owners.
6. Offer one low-cost diagnostic call.
7. Convert the strongest problem into a project proposal.

## Starter Prompt

```text
Act as an AI automation consultant. My target business type is [BUSINESS]. List ten repetitive workflows they likely have. Pick the three with the clearest ROI. Create an audit offer, a cold email, and a simple proposal outline.
```

## Delivery Checklist

- Business niche
- Repetitive workflow
- Time saved
- Clear audit offer
- Simple implementation scope
- Monthly support option
