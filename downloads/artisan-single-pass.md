# HugoMojo Artisan Single Pass

## Role Signal

The Artisan is built for knowledge packaging, product creation, and digital asset monetization.

## First 7 Days

1. Pick one topic where you have real taste, experience, or research depth.
2. List five painful problems in that topic.
3. Choose one problem that can be solved with a short guide, template, or prompt pack.
4. Create the product outline.
5. Draft the first version with AI.
6. Package the product page.
7. Publish the first version and gather feedback.

## Starter Prompt

```text
Act as a digital product strategist. I know about [TOPIC]. My audience is [AUDIENCE]. Generate five small product ideas that solve a specific painful problem. For each idea, include title, promise, contents, price range, and first launch channel.
```

## Delivery Checklist

- Specific audience
- Specific problem
- Small product scope
- Clear before-and-after promise
- Simple landing page
- First feedback loop
