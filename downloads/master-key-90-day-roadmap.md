# HugoMojo Master Key 90-Day Roadmap

## Phase 1: Signal

Days 1 to 14 focus on finding the fastest proof of demand.

- Choose one primary path.
- Create one offer or product.
- Contact or publish to a small test audience.
- Track replies, clicks, and objections.

## Phase 2: System

Days 15 to 45 focus on repeatable delivery.

- Build a checklist.
- Save reusable prompts.
- Define a simple pricing ladder.
- Create a weekly review cadence.

## Phase 3: Scale

Days 46 to 90 focus on compounding the best signal.

- Double down on the path with real response.
- Cut paths with no signal.
- Package proof into a case study.
- Decide whether to stay at a single report, continue with the Master Key path, or use Annual Access support.

## Monthly Update Slots

- Month 1: First opportunity gap briefing.
- Month 2: Tool stack update.
- Month 3: Path correction and next product recommendation.

## Disclaimer

This roadmap is educational and strategic. It does not guarantee income.
