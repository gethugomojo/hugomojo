AI Income Path Report

A focused action system for turning your current resources into a clear first market test.

THE ARTISAN

Packaging knowledge, taste, creative judgment, or niche experience into a sellable digital asset.

01 · YOUR MATCH

You matched as an Artisan.

PATH LOGIC

The Artisan path is for people who are better at packaging knowledge, taste, creative judgment, or niche experience into a sellable digital asset.

CORE DIAGNOSIS

Your fastest path is not doing random AI tasks. It is turning what you already know into a small paid asset.

02 · TODAY'S PRIORITY
Ask 10 people if the problem is real. Not a brand launch. Just signal.

The Artisan path starts with a useful asset, not a perfect brand.

03 · SIGNALS DETECTED
✓You have a topic, skill, taste, or experience base that can be packaged.
✓You prefer assets over constant client communication.
✓You may already have some audience, expertise, or creative judgment.
✓You are willing to build before the first sale.
✓You are more patient than a Hunter, but still need a focused first product.
✓You can use AI to speed up writing, structuring, design, and packaging.
04 · MARKET FIT MODEL
SHORTCUT

People buy when a product helps them complete a task faster.

TEMPLATE

They buy a reusable structure when they repeat the same problem often.

SPECIFIC ANSWER

The market does not need another broad ebook. It needs a specific product for a specific person.

Good Artisan products are usually small: a prompt pack, checklist, guide, Notion template, spreadsheet calculator, swipe file, mini SOP, or Canva template pack.

05 · YOUR FIRST OFFER
OFFER NAME
Small Useful Asset
DELIVERABLES
  • One checklist, template, mini-guide, prompt pack, or niche asset pack.
  • Simple PDF, Notion page, spreadsheet, or Canva file.
  • One product page with a clear promise and access note.
SUGGESTED STARTER PRICING

$9-$29 for version 1, depending on usefulness and audience. This is a starting reference, not an income promise.

06 · PRODUCT OPTIONS
OPTION A

Prompt Pack

Best if your topic involves repeatable thinking, writing, planning, or decision-making.

OPTION B

Practical Guide

Best if you can explain a process clearly and turn it into a 7-day path.

OPTION C

Template

Best if your audience repeats the same task often and needs a usable structure.

Pick a product that can be built in 7 days. If it takes longer, the scope is too large.

07 · TOOL STACK
CREATE
  • Claude or ChatGPT for outlines, drafting, prompt creation, and product copy.
  • Canva for PDF layout, cover, product mockups, and simple templates.
  • Google Docs or Notion for drafting and internal structure.
TEST
  • Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, or Creem for simple checkout.
  • X, Reddit, LinkedIn, Etsy, or niche communities for first feedback.
  • Do not build a website first. Build the asset and one clear product page.
08 · 7-DAY SPRINT
□ Day 1 · Pick one audience and one repeated problem.
□ Day 2 · Outline the smallest useful asset.
□ Day 3 · Draft version 1 with AI assistance.
□ Day 4 · Add examples, instructions, and a clear use case.
□ Day 5 · Create one simple product page.
□ Day 6 · Ask 10 relevant people for feedback.
□ Day 7 · Revise the title, promise, and first section based on feedback.
09 · 30-DAY ACTION PLAN
WEEK 1

Find the product problem: pick one audience, list 10 annoying tasks, choose one task, and write the product promise in one sentence.

WEEK 2

Build version 1: draft the outline, use AI to create the first version, add examples, and package it simply.

WEEK 3

Publish and test: create one product page, share it in one channel, and ask for feedback from 10 relevant people.

WEEK 4

Improve the asset: fix unclear sections, add one missing example, and decide whether to keep, repackage, or start a new asset.

10 · PROMPTS AND TEMPLATES
PRODUCT IDEA PROMPT
Act as a digital product strategist.

I know about:
[TOPIC]

My likely audience:
[AUDIENCE]

Generate 10 small digital product ideas.

For each idea include:
1. Product title
2. Specific audience
3. Painful problem solved
4. Product format
5. What is included
6. Price range
7. First place to test it
FEEDBACK REQUEST TEMPLATE
Hi [NAME] - I made a small resource for [AUDIENCE] who struggle with [PROBLEM].

It is a [FORMAT] that helps them [OUTCOME].

Could I send you the page and ask one question: does the promise feel useful, or is it too vague?
11 · RESOURCE GAP WARNINGS
✓Product too broad. A product for everyone is usually bought by no one.
✓Building for months without feedback.
✓Over-designing before proving demand.
✓Avoiding distribution. Products do not sell just because they exist.
