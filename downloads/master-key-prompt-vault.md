# HugoMojo Master Key Prompt Vault

## Universal Path Selector

```text
You are my AI income path strategist. Compare these three paths for me: AI freelancing, digital products, and B2B AI automation. Use my resources: time [TIME], skills [SKILLS], budget [BUDGET], audience [AUDIENCE], English level [ENGLISH], and AI experience [AI_LEVEL]. Score each path from 1 to 10 and give me the best first move.
```

## Hunter Offer Builder

```text
Turn my skill [SKILL] into a simple AI-assisted service offer for [CUSTOMER]. Include outcome, deliverables, price ladder, proof asset, and 20 outreach targets.
```

## Artisan Product Builder

```text
Create a small digital product plan around [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. Include product promise, outline, asset list, sales page sections, and 7-day launch plan.
```

## Architect Audit Builder

```text
Create a B2B AI audit offer for [BUSINESS_TYPE]. Identify three workflows to automate, estimate time saved, write a cold email, and outline a paid pilot project.
```

## Weekly Review Prompt

```text
Review my last 7 days of execution: [NOTES]. Identify what worked, what failed, what to stop, what to repeat, and the single next action with the highest expected return.
```
